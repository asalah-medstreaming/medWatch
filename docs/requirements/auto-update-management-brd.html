1. Executive Summary

This BRD captures prioritized business requirements for auto-update management in the Medstreaming internal Operations Monitoring Platform.

2. Objectives and Assumptions

Objective: establish measurable operational outcomes with reusable monitoring domains and healthcare-ready examples (HL7, PACS, interface services).

3. Business Requirements

Requirement IDRequirement StatementUser Story LinksBacklog Links
AUM-REQ-001The platform shall monitor auto-update management with ownership mapping and health state visibility.StoriesTasks
AUM-REQ-002The platform shall support threshold-based alerting, severity, and escalation aligned to operations policy.StoriesTasks
AUM-REQ-003The platform shall maintain auditable history, runbook reference, and retention policy for operational decisions.StoriesTasks

4. Business Rules and Constraints

  • Role-based access controls for operational actions.
  • Audit trail for rule, alert, incident, and remediation changes.
  • Maintenance windows suppress non-actionable noise.
  • Safe automation governance and explicit approvals for risky actions.
