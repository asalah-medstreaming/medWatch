Executive Summary

This backlog page now uses the MVP-refactored titles and enhanced delivery descriptions so each task clearly states the business objective, scope, implementation guidance, expected outcome, sprint, and dependency path.

Sprint and Phase

Sprint 2 · Phase 2 of 6 · Core platform monitoring for applications, HL7/PACS exchanges, uptime, and workload flow.

Business Value

Medstreaming gains broader risk detection across critical service pathways that affect operational continuity.

Delivered Capability

Operations teams can assess application, exchange, availability, and processing flow health across core services.

Demonstration Scenario

A shift supervisor reviews overnight reliability, detects slowed exchange activity, and assigns follow-up actions before incidents escalate.

QMON-TASK-001 Deliver Workload Processing Flow Visibility Governance

Traceability: Sprint 2 → MVP → QMON-REQ-001 → QMON-US-001

  • Business Objective: Prevent processing delays by exposing backlog growth and throughput bottlenecks in operational workflows.
  • Scope: Include oversight practices, evidence retention expectations, and operating guidance to sustain reliable daily use.
  • Implementation Guidance: Develop a system that records activity history, preserves required evidence, and guides teams through repeatable operations.
  • Expected Outcome: Operations teams can run the capability confidently with traceable oversight and repeatable execution.
  • Sprint: Sprint 2
  • Phase: Core Platform
  • Status: MVP
  • Dependencies:
    • Provide Workload Processing Flow Visibility Control Policies
