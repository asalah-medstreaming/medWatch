Executive Summary

This backlog page now uses the MVP-refactored titles and enhanced delivery descriptions so each task clearly states the business objective, scope, implementation guidance, expected outcome, sprint, and dependency path.

Sprint and Phase

Sprint 4 · Phase 4 of 6 · Automation governance, maintenance controls, and safe remediation.

Business Value

Medstreaming improves consistency and response speed by reducing manual handling of known conditions.

Delivered Capability

Operations teams can apply policy-driven automation and approved corrective actions to recurring issues.

Demonstration Scenario

When repeated storage pressure occurs, the team uses approved automated correction and verifies completion through governed activity records.

MWM-TASK-001 Deliver Planned Change Risk Control Governance

Traceability: Sprint 4 → MVP → MWM-REQ-001 → MWM-US-001

  • Business Objective: Reduce planned-change risk by coordinating maintenance periods with clear timing and communication controls.
  • Scope: Include oversight practices, evidence retention expectations, and operating guidance to sustain reliable daily use.
  • Implementation Guidance: Develop a system that records activity history, preserves required evidence, and guides teams through repeatable operations.
  • Expected Outcome: Operations teams can run the capability confidently with traceable oversight and repeatable execution.
  • Sprint: Sprint 4
  • Phase: Automation
  • Status: MVP
