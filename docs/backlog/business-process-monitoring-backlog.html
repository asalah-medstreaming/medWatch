Executive Summary

This backlog page now uses the MVP-refactored titles and enhanced delivery descriptions so each task clearly states the business objective, scope, implementation guidance, expected outcome, sprint, and dependency path.

Sprint and Phase

Sprint 5 · Phase 5 of 6 · Presentation and reporting for trend analysis and workflow reliability.

Business Value

Medstreaming gains trend visibility to prioritize reliability improvements and operational planning decisions.

Delivered Capability

Demonstration Scenario

At monthly operations review, leadership uses trend reports to identify recurring instability and prioritize targeted improvement actions.

Traceability: Sprint 5 → MVP → BPM-REQ-001 → BPM-US-001
