1. Executive Summary

This MVP scope defines must-have and later-scope priorities for rule engine management.

2. Must-Have Scope

  • Core capability outcomes and health visibility
  • Threshold and incident-ready response workflow
  • Audit, access, and maintenance-window alignment

3. Should-Have / Later Scope

  • Advanced correlation and predictive insights
  • Expanded automation and richer reporting depth

4. MVP Exclusions

  • Commercial product packaging
  • Non-essential complex integrations

5. Dependencies and Success Measures

  • Dependency: defined ownership and runbook references
  • Dependency: agreed severity and escalation policy
  • Success: reduced mean time to detect and triage
