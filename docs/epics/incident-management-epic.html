Executive Summary

Incident Management aligns the MVP backlog to business outcomes instead of generic implementation wording. This epic packages governance, setup, and operating policy work for structured incident ownership so Medstreaming can deliver measurable capability in Sprint 3.

Sprint Mapping

Sprint 3 · Activate coordinated response through alerting, notifications, incident ownership, and investigation support.

Phase

Operational

Business Value

Medstreaming reduces time-to-response with a structured communication and incident workflow.

Delivered Capability

Operations teams can receive urgent notifications, coordinate incidents, and investigate issues from shared evidence.

Enhanced Delivery Themes

INC-REQ-001 Deliver Structured Incident Ownership Governance

  • Business Objective: Improve resolution outcomes by managing incidents with clear ownership, status tracking, and closure discipline.
  • Scope: Include oversight practices, evidence retention expectations, and operating guidance to sustain reliable daily use.
  • Implementation Guidance: Develop a system that records activity history, preserves required evidence, and guides teams through repeatable operations.
  • Expected Outcome: Operations teams can run the capability confidently with traceable oversight and repeatable execution.
  • Sprint: Sprint 3
  • Phase: Operational
  • Status: MVP
  • Dependencies:
    • Provide Structured Incident Ownership Control Policies

INC-REQ-002 Enable Structured Incident Ownership Setup

  • Business Objective: Improve resolution outcomes by managing incidents with clear ownership, status tracking, and closure discipline.
  • Scope: Create the initial structured incident ownership capability by defining participating assets, ownership assignments, and usage boundaries.
  • Implementation Guidance: Create a mechanism that captures operational context, ownership, and classification information for controlled use.
  • Expected Outcome: Operations teams can start using structured incident ownership with clear accountability and consistent setup standards.
  • Sprint: Sprint 3
  • Phase: Operational
  • Status: MVP
  • Dependencies:
    • Enable Trusted Monitoring Asset Enrollment Setup
    • Enable Consistent Operational Data Intake Setup

INC-REQ-003 Provide Structured Incident Ownership Control Policies

  • Business Objective: Improve resolution outcomes by managing incidents with clear ownership, status tracking, and closure discipline.
  • Scope: Include business rules for prioritization, severity handling, and response timing so teams can act consistently.
  • Implementation Guidance: Establish a process that applies configurable decision criteria and escalation pathways to operational conditions.
  • Expected Outcome: Operations teams can prioritize work using agreed decision rules and respond with consistent urgency.
  • Sprint: Sprint 3
  • Phase: Operational
  • Status: MVP
  • Dependencies:
    • Enable Structured Incident Ownership Setup
