1. Executive Summary

AI Management enables Medstreaming operations teams to maintain reliable internal monitoring with reusable capability patterns and healthcare-aware examples.

2. Purpose and Scope

This epic defines MVP outcomes, emphasizing operational visibility, alert quality, and governed response workflows.

3. Business Value

  • Faster detection and triage
  • Improved service reliability and ownership clarity
  • Reduced alert noise and safer automation

4. In-Scope MVP Capabilities

  • Core ai management outcomes
  • Role-based access and auditability
  • Maintenance-window awareness and runbook references

5. Out of Scope (MVP)

  • Advanced predictive optimization
  • Complex external billing/commercial features

6. Stakeholders

Operations analysts, support engineers, incident managers, platform administrators, compliance reviewers.
