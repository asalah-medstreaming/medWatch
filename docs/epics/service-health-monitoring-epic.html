Executive Summary

Service Health Monitoring aligns the MVP backlog to business outcomes instead of generic implementation wording. This epic packages governance, setup, and operating policy work for application service health visibility so Medstreaming can deliver measurable capability in Sprint 2.

Sprint Mapping

Sprint 2 · Expand visibility to service reliability, availability, data exchange continuity, and processing flow health.

Phase

Core Platform

Business Value

Medstreaming gains broader risk detection across critical service pathways that affect operational continuity.

Delivered Capability

Operations teams can assess application, exchange, availability, and processing flow health across core services.

Enhanced Delivery Themes

SHM-REQ-001 Deliver Application Service Health Visibility Governance

  • Business Objective: Show whether business applications and critical integrations are functioning as expected for clinical operations.
  • Scope: Include oversight practices, evidence retention expectations, and operating guidance to sustain reliable daily use.
  • Implementation Guidance: Develop a system that records activity history, preserves required evidence, and guides teams through repeatable operations.
  • Expected Outcome: Operations teams can run the capability confidently with traceable oversight and repeatable execution.
  • Sprint: Sprint 2
  • Phase: Core Platform
  • Status: MVP
  • Dependencies:
    • Provide Application Service Health Visibility Control Policies

SHM-REQ-002 Enable Application Service Health Visibility Setup

  • Business Objective: Show whether business applications and critical integrations are functioning as expected for clinical operations.
  • Scope: Create the initial application service health visibility capability by defining participating assets, ownership assignments, and usage boundaries.
  • Implementation Guidance: Create a mechanism that captures operational context, ownership, and classification information for controlled use.
  • Expected Outcome: Operations teams can start using application service health visibility with clear accountability and consistent setup standards.
  • Sprint: Sprint 2
  • Phase: Core Platform
  • Status: MVP
  • Dependencies:
    • Enable Trusted Monitoring Asset Enrollment Setup
    • Enable Consistent Operational Data Intake Setup

SHM-REQ-003 Provide Application Service Health Visibility Control Policies

  • Business Objective: Show whether business applications and critical integrations are functioning as expected for clinical operations.
  • Scope: Include business rules for prioritization, severity handling, and response timing so teams can act consistently.
  • Implementation Guidance: Establish a process that applies configurable decision criteria and escalation pathways to operational conditions.
  • Expected Outcome: Operations teams can prioritize work using agreed decision rules and respond with consistent urgency.
  • Sprint: Sprint 2
  • Phase: Core Platform
  • Status: MVP
  • Dependencies:
    • Enable Application Service Health Visibility Setup
