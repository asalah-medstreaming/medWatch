Executive Summary

Business Process Monitoring aligns the MVP backlog to business outcomes instead of generic implementation wording. This epic packages governance, setup, and operating policy work for business workflow reliability visibility so Medstreaming can deliver measurable capability in Sprint 5.

Sprint Mapping

Sprint 5 · Deliver historical insight and workflow performance reporting for continuous improvement planning.

Phase

Presentation

Business Value

Medstreaming gains trend visibility to prioritize reliability improvements and operational planning decisions.

Delivered Capability

Operations teams can review performance trends and workflow reliability to guide improvement priorities.

Enhanced Delivery Themes

BPM-REQ-001 Deliver Business Workflow Reliability Visibility Governance

  • Business Objective: Reveal workflow interruptions early so patient-facing and internal business processes stay on track.
  • Scope: Include oversight practices, evidence retention expectations, and operating guidance to sustain reliable daily use.
  • Implementation Guidance: Develop a system that records activity history, preserves required evidence, and guides teams through repeatable operations.
  • Expected Outcome: Operations teams can run the capability confidently with traceable oversight and repeatable execution.
  • Sprint: Sprint 5
  • Phase: Presentation
  • Status: MVP
  • Dependencies:
    • Provide Business Workflow Reliability Visibility Control Policies

BPM-REQ-002 Enable Business Workflow Reliability Visibility Setup

  • Business Objective: Reveal workflow interruptions early so patient-facing and internal business processes stay on track.
  • Scope: Create the initial business workflow reliability visibility capability by defining participating assets, ownership assignments, and usage boundaries.
  • Implementation Guidance: Create a mechanism that captures operational context, ownership, and classification information for controlled use.
  • Expected Outcome: Operations teams can start using business workflow reliability visibility with clear accountability and consistent setup standards.
  • Sprint: Sprint 5
  • Phase: Presentation
  • Status: MVP
  • Dependencies:
    • Enable Trusted Monitoring Asset Enrollment Setup
    • Enable Consistent Operational Data Intake Setup

BPM-REQ-003 Provide Business Workflow Reliability Visibility Control Policies

  • Business Objective: Reveal workflow interruptions early so patient-facing and internal business processes stay on track.
  • Scope: Include business rules for prioritization, severity handling, and response timing so teams can act consistently.
  • Implementation Guidance: Establish a process that applies configurable decision criteria and escalation pathways to operational conditions.
  • Expected Outcome: Operations teams can prioritize work using agreed decision rules and respond with consistent urgency.
  • Sprint: Sprint 5
  • Phase: Presentation
  • Status: MVP
  • Dependencies:
    • Enable Business Workflow Reliability Visibility Setup
