Project Overview

MedWatch is an internal Medstreaming monitoring MVP focused on infrastructure, application service health, HL7/PACS exchange visibility, alerting, incidents, governed automation, and continuous operational improvement.

Sprint Planning Dashboard

Use the sprint planning view to understand phase sequencing, business value delivery, demonstration scenarios, and traceability across epics, requirements, stories, and backlog tasks.

Open the Sprint Planning page Open the implementation-ready backlog

Sprint 1: Foundation

Goal: Enable first usable monitoring capability with ownership setup and initial operational visibility.

Business Value: Medstreaming gains immediate operational transparency while foundational controls are established in the same sprint.

Sprint 2: Core Platform

Goal: Expand visibility to service reliability, availability, data exchange continuity, and processing flow health.

Business Value: Medstreaming gains broader risk detection across critical service pathways that affect operational continuity.

Sprint 3: Operational

Goal: Activate coordinated response through alerting, notifications, incident ownership, and investigation support.

Business Value: Medstreaming reduces time-to-response with a structured communication and incident workflow.

Sprint 4: Automation

Goal: Introduce policy-driven automation and approved corrective actions for recurring issues.

Business Value: Medstreaming improves consistency and response speed by reducing manual handling of known conditions.

Sprint 5: Presentation

Goal: Deliver historical insight and workflow performance reporting for continuous improvement planning.

Business Value: Medstreaming gains trend visibility to prioritize reliability improvements and operational planning decisions.

Sprint 6: Advanced

Goal: Deliver intelligent guidance and continuous solution currency to sustain mature operations.

Business Value: Medstreaming maintains operational readiness with lower manual effort and faster adaptation to changing conditions.

Sprint Phases

  1. Sprint 1Foundation
    Phase 1 of 6 · Foundation controls, asset enrollment, and first-line visibility.
  2. Sprint 2Core Platform
    Phase 2 of 6 · Core platform monitoring for applications, HL7/PACS exchanges, uptime, and workload flow.
  3. Sprint 3Operational
    Phase 3 of 6 · Operational response workflows, evidence, and collaboration.
  4. Sprint 4Automation
    Phase 4 of 6 · Automation governance, maintenance controls, and safe remediation.
  5. Sprint 5Presentation
    Phase 5 of 6 · Presentation and reporting for trend analysis and workflow reliability.
  6. Sprint 6Advanced
    Phase 6 of 6 · Advanced guidance and continuous update readiness.
