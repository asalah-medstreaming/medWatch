Sprint Planning Overview

This page consolidates all sprint goals, business value, dependencies, delivered capabilities, and demonstration scenarios for the MVP-refactored backlog. It is the primary planning view for sequencing work across foundation, core platform, operational, automation, presentation, and advanced phases.

Phase Timeline

  1. Sprint 1Foundation
    Enable first usable monitoring capability with ownership setup and initial operational visibility.
  2. Sprint 2Core Platform
    Expand visibility to service reliability, availability, data exchange continuity, and processing flow health.
  3. Sprint 3Operational
    Activate coordinated response through alerting, notifications, incident ownership, and investigation support.
  4. Sprint 4Automation
    Introduce policy-driven automation and approved corrective actions for recurring issues.
  5. Sprint 5Presentation
    Deliver historical insight and workflow performance reporting for continuous improvement planning.
  6. Sprint 6Advanced
    Deliver intelligent guidance and continuous solution currency to sustain mature operations.
